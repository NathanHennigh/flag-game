Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

World Flags is a flag quiz and flashcard app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information We Collect

If you create an account or sign in, we collect the information needed to authenticate you, such as your email address and basic profile information provided by your sign-in provider. If you use Google sign-in, Google may provide your name, email address, and profile image depending on your Google account settings.

Quiz and Practice Data

The app may store quiz scores, flashcard review history, and practice preferences. In the current version, this progress data is stored locally in your browser and separated by account when you sign in.

How We Use Information

  • To let you sign in and manage your account session.
  • To save and display your quiz and flashcard progress.
  • To improve the app experience and keep your practice data associated with your profile.

Third-Party Services

World Flags uses Supabase for authentication and may use Google as a sign-in provider. These services process authentication information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information with service providers needed to operate authentication and app functionality.

Data Retention

Authentication data is retained as needed to maintain your account. Local quiz and flashcard data remains in your browser unless you clear your browser storage or the app provides a deletion option.

Your Choices

You can sign out at any time. You can also clear local app data through your browser settings. If you want account data deleted, contact the app owner.

Children's Privacy

World Flags is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact the app owner so it can be reviewed and removed where appropriate.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.
